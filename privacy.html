Ramble — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Ramble is a voice recording app designed with privacy at its core. This policy explains what data the app accesses, where it goes, and what control you have over it.

What data does Ramble access?

Ramble accesses your device's microphone to record voice notes. If you use the Apple Watch companion app, it also records audio on-watch and transfers recordings to your iPhone. Ramble does not access your contacts, location, camera, or any other device sensors.

Where is my data stored?

All recordings and transcriptions are stored locally on your device. Audio files are saved to the app's private storage on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party platform. There is no Ramble backend, no database, and no server infrastructure.

Does Ramble collect any personal information?

No. Ramble does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no email addresses, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. Ramble does not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting services.

Does Ramble share data with third parties?

No. Your data never leaves your device. Ramble has no mechanism to share, sell, or transmit your recordings or any other data to any third party.

Transcription

Ramble uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework to transcribe your recordings. This processing happens entirely on your device. No audio is sent to external servers for transcription.

How can I delete my data?

You can delete individual recordings within the app at any time. To remove all data, simply delete the Ramble app from your device — all recordings and app data will be permanently removed.

Children's privacy

Ramble does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from anyone — it stores recordings locally on the user's own device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. As Ramble does not collect email addresses or have user accounts, this page is the sole means of communicating policy updates.
