Ramble

Capture your thoughts before they slip away

Ramble is a pocket-sized voice recorder that helps you hold onto fleeting thoughts throughout the day. At the end of the day, sync with your phone to automatically transcribe and organize everything into your own custom categories—ideas, to-dos, recipes, names, or anything else you dream up.

What It Is

We have over 50,000 thoughts every day, but most of them vanish before we can do anything with them. Ramble is a simple voice recorder you keep in your pocket that captures those thoughts the moment they happen—no need to pull out your phone and break your stride.

At the end of the day, sync it with your phone. Everything gets automatically transcribed, and you can sort your thoughts into custom categories: To-Do List, Recipes, Ideas, Names, Coaching Tips, Quotes from Podcasts—whatever makes sense for you. Finally, a way to actually remember and analyze what goes through your head.

How It Works

  • Quick-access button to record thoughts instantly
  • High-quality microphone captures clear audio
  • Stores recordings locally on SD card
  • Syncs with your phone at the end of the day
  • Automatic transcription of all recordings
  • Sort into custom categories (Ideas, To-Dos, Recipes, etc.)
  • Rechargeable battery lasts all day
  • Small enough to keep in your pocket

Simple App, Powerful Organization

After you sync, the app transcribes your voice notes and lets you organize them however makes sense to you. Create categories, search through your thoughts, and never lose an idea again.

Note: App interface is a work in progress and subject to change.
